SUMMARY (EN)
Contemporary hotel with ~55 rooms/suites focused on comfort and design. Features indoor/outdoor heated pool, dry and wet saunas, Scottish showers, hydromassage, gym, and a garden-view bar serving breakfast. Spacious rooms; some with balcony, garden views, or sea views.

ROOMS (EN)
- Standard (~22 m²)
  - Bedding: 2 twins or 1 King; connecting rooms option for families; some with balcony
  - Amenities: Wi‑Fi, 4K TV, workspace, AC, minibar, Scottish shower, room service, non‑smoking
- Superior (~26–40 m²)
  - King bed; garden views. Some with balcony. ~40 m² units may allow extra bed (triple occupancy)
  - Amenities: Wi‑Fi, 4K TV, AC, minibar, Scottish shower, room service, non‑smoking
- Junior Suite (~38 m²)
  - Two distinct spaces: bedroom with King + lounge with a two‑seater sofa bed, independent
  - Amenities: Wi‑Fi, 4K TV, AC, minibar, Scottish shower, room service, non‑smoking
- Master Suites (~39–45 m²)
  - Sector A; sea views, large balcony; whirlpool bathtub. In some cases two King beds (2x2 m) and larger TV
  - Amenities: Wi‑Fi, AC, minibar, Scottish shower, room service, non‑smoking
